## 6. METHODOLOGY — Structured Intervention Approach

Present as a 5-step process:

### Step 1: Gap Assessment
- Audit performed based on criteria agreed with the customer
- Standards: IATF 16949, VDA 6.3, CQI, GM 1927, GM 30, 9+2, Q1, etc.
- Current situation documented

### Step 2: Risk Identification
- Highest risks identified from assessment
- Containment actions immediately implemented
- Action plan elaborated and agreed with the organization
- Resources allocated

### Step 3: On-Site Support Activation
- Consultant assigned to site
- Working teams formed with supplier personnel
- Responsible owners assigned for each action
- Training for Top Management and key contributors

### Step 4: Execution & Monitoring
- Weekly reports of actions implemented
- Roadblocks, issues, and risks communicated proactively
- Effectiveness monitored through agreed key metrics

### Step 5: Validation & Stability
- Certificate recovered?
- Sourceability Level acceptable?
- Business on Hold removed?
- CS1/CS2 removed?
- If yes → Optional: proactive/preventive QMS continuous improvement plan

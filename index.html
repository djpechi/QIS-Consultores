How We Work

Structured Intervention
Approach

Our proven 5-step methodology ensures systematic recovery — from initial assessment to validated stability. No shortcuts, no guesswork.

1

Gap Assessment

Audit performed based on criteria agreed with the customer — IATF 16949, VDA 6.3, CQI, GM 1927, GM 30, 9+2, Q1. Current situation fully documented.

2

Risk Identification

Highest risks identified. Containment actions immediately implemented. Action plan elaborated and agreed with the organization. Resources allocated.

3

On-Site Support Activation

Consultant assigned to site. Working teams formed with supplier personnel. Responsible owners assigned. Training for Top Management and key contributors.

4

Execution & Monitoring

Weekly reports of actions implemented. Roadblocks, issues, and risks communicated proactively. Effectiveness monitored through agreed key metrics.

5

Validation & Stability

IATF 16949 certificate recovered? Supplier performance level acceptable? New Business On Hold removed? External inspection and sorting activities removed? If yes — optional proactive QMS continuous improvement plan.
